How To Play
Setup
  • Create an account
  • Join a league
  • Set your lineup for the week

Playing the Game
  • Every week you'll choose 2 quarterbacks, 2 running backs, 2 wide receivers, 1 flex, and 1 kicker from a list of EVERY player in college football
  • Your goal is to get the most cumulative points throughout the season in your league to win!
